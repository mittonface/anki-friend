**はやい** — fast (in terms of time)

### Example

A「東京から大阪まで2時間半です。」<br>B「<b>早い</b>ですね。」

A: It takes two and a half hours from Tokyo to Osaka.
B: That's fast, isn't it?

### Notes

When fast is related to (physical) speed, we write it 速い. 

Fast karate chop, hayai
