**Keyword:** flower

### Primitive

We are not yet equipped with all the pieces necessary to learn the character for flower, so shall have to content ourselves here with
the first three strokes, which represent the primitive of the same meaning. Concentrate on the actual "bloom" of the flower, and
keep a particular flower in mind. Try a rose, a tulip, or a daisy, since none of them will have their own kanji. Think about it well,
since once you have decided on your flower of choice, you will be using it in a rather large number of stories later on.
