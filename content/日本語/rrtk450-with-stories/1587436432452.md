**Keyword:** ten

### Story

Two needles crossing marks the spot, or you can use the Roman character for ten (rotated 45 degrees).

I scared away ten vampires with a single cross!

### Primitive

As a primitive, this character sometimes keeps its meaning of ten and sometimes signifies needle, this latter derived from the kanji for needle (針).
