### Story

The samurai's wife gives him a (uhum) before battle for good luck.

A true samurai gentleman will always mouth, "Good luck" to his opponents before a match.

### Primitive

As a primitive, we shall take this shape to mean an aerosol can, from the mouth and the very tightly-fitting lid (note how it differs here from the lidded crock).
