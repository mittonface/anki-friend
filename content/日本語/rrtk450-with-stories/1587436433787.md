**Keyword:** someone

**Meanings:** person

### Story

I find the best way of memorizing this kanji is in conjunction with the Japanese compound for ninja: 忍者 -- i.e. someone who endures (see FRAME 597 for the kanji 忍). A 忍者 is not just a 身 (somebody), it's 者 who can 忍 great 拷!

Back in the day, the old man used to be someone. His name was in the papers and all that. I use CHUCKY from the movie Child's Play as my major alternate meaning for this primitive.

### Primitive

As a primitive it means a puppet-on-a-string.
