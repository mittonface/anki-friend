### Story

On the left you see the rock, which is familiar enough. But pay attention to the right. Taking careful note of the unusual stroke order that has the "chimney" on the house doubled up with the first stroke of the turkey, we may see the right side as a turkey house (or "coop").

To assure that no one will get into the Turkey coop, put a huge rock in the entrance!

You've heard the expression "killing two birds with one stone." (一石二鳥 is-seki-ni-chou). We'll, there's another one that roughly translates as "killing an entire coop with a giant boulder. " That's the method that hunters use when they want absolute assurance of taking home a bird.
