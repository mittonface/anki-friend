### Story

Ok, this is where knowing some Eastern culture comes in handy! In Japanese society, when someone dies they creamate the body and pick out the remaining bones with special chopsticks. In this case, they're out of chopsticks so they decide to use (gasp!) a spoon instead.

Imagine the grim reaper (the bringer of death), carrying a spoon made of human bones instead of a scythe.
