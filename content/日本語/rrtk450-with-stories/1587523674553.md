**Keyword:** quarter

### Primitive

This character simply splits the vertical stroke of a half in half once again, to get a quarter. In so doing, it spreads the split stroke
out to form a sort of enclosure under which its main relative primitive will be placed. It can be used either in its substantive or
verbal meaning.
