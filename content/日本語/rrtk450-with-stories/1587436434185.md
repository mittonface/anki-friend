**Keyword:** gates

### Story

Heisig's stroke order in the 4th edition (and earlier) is wrong, so be careful -- first is the left-most vertical stroke, then draw the rest of the "sun" attachment as you would normally. In fact, if you just wrote it out as two suns with elongated sides, you'll get the stroke order exactly correct.

The gates to Hell. guarded by Cerebus or ... Bill Gates?

### Primitive

As a primitive, we shall continue to give it the meaning of gates, but recommend the image of swinging doors (like the kind
once common at entrances to saloons) to distinguish it from the primitive for door.
