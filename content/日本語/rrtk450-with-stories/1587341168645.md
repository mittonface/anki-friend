**Keyword:** taskmaster

### Primitive

First find the long rod (the first stroke), held in the hand of someone seated (the next 3 strokes, not unlike the pictograph for
woman (女), but quite different from that for walking legs (夂). The only thing left to do is conjure up the memory
of some taskmaster (or taskmistress) from your past whom you will "never forget."
