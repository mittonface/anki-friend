### Story

Pictograph, see book. * As a primitive, using the image of going through a door, even one that stands alone with the frame and no wall around, helps flesh out the primitive "enter".

Person enters stage left…

### Primitive

As a primitive, the meaning of the key word is expanded to include: to go in, to put in, to come in, and the like. It generally
appears atop its relative primitive, where, unlike the element for umbrella, the two strokes do not touch each other,
making it virtually the same as the kanji for eight. When it appears in any other position, however, it retains its original form.
