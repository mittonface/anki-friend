### Story

The sign of the bird on this whiskey bottle is of course the Famous Grouse blended Scotch.

Normally birds fly south, but one bird flew west. People were worried about it and kept asking "Is there any sign of the bird – you know, the one that flew west?".

### Primitive

As a primitive, it means whiskey bottle. In its pictograph, you can see the loosely corked lid, the bottle, and the contents
(about one-third full). You might also think of the Spanish "porrón," a decanter shaped like a long-necked bird.
