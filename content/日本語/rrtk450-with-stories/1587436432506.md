**Keyword:** page

**Meanings:** leaf

### Story

A shellfish carrying a page balanced on its head.

One drop of ink from a shellfish can fill a whole page.

### Primitive

As a primitive, this kanji takes the unrelated meaning of a head (preferably one detached from its body), derived from the character for head (頭).
