**Keyword:** hole

**Meanings:** aperture, slit, cave, den

### Story

A black hole is a "House of Infinity". Infinity is of course represented by a sideways "8", but here we have a Japanese "8" instead.

Billiards anyone? House rules here state that the first person to pot the eight ball into the hole wins the game.

### Primitive

As a primitive, this kanji uses an alternate form: the primitive for eight is replaced with that for human legs.
