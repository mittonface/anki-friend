### Primitive

Here we see a pictograph of a long and slightly warped arrow. By extending the short final stroke in both directions, you should see
the arrowhead without any difficulty. The hook at the bottom represents the feathers at the butt end. When it serves as a semi-
enclosure for other primitives, the first stroke is drawn longer, as we shall see in the following frames.
