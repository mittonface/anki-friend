### Primitive

The picture in this primitive is what we may call a "tassled arrow." A decorative tassle is strung on the shaft of an arrow to indicate
that it is no longer a weapon but a symbol of a fiesta. As before, the first stroke is extended when it serves as a semi-enclosure.
