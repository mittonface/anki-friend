**Keyword:** silver

### Primitive

We give this element the meaning of silver from the kanji "銀". Both the original pictographic representation and
the primitive elements that make it up are more trouble to hunt out than they are worth. It is best simply to learn it as is. In doing
so, take careful note of the stroke order, and also the fact that when this element appears on the left, the penultimate stroke is
omitted, giving us simply .
