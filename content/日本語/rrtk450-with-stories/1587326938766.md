**Keyword:** animal legs

### Primitive

Like the four that follow it, this primitive is not a kanji in its own right, though it is said to be derived from 八, the character we
learned earlier for eight. It always comes at the bottom of the primitive to which it is related.
It can mean the legs of any kind of
animal: from a grizzly bear's paws to an octopus's tentacles to the spindle shanks of a spider. (The one animal not allowed is our
friend homo sapiens.) Even where the term "legs" will apply metaphorically to the legs of
pieces of furniture, it is best to keep the association with animal legs.
