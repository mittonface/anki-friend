**Keyword:** self

**Meanings:** snake, serpent

### Story

This kanji looks like the "s" of "self", looking at its own reflection in a mirror (reversed sideways), as if pondering about his "self".

When Superman looks at himself in the mirror, this is what he sees on his chest.

### Primitive

As a primitive element, this kanji can be used for the snake—of which it is a pictograph—or any of the various concrete symbolic meanings the snake has in myth and fable.
